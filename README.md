# Sta. Lucia High School Chatbot (Lucy)

The **Sta. Lucia High School Chatbot**, named *Lucy*, is a web-based conversational AI developed using Google AI Studio and the Gemini API. It is designed to provide quick, accurate, and user-friendly access to school-related information for the Sta. Lucia High School (SLHS) community in Pasig City.

---

## Features

- Answers inquiries about academic programs and strands.  
- Provides information on enrollment procedures and requirements.  
- Supplies details about faculty and staff.  
- Responds to questions on student policies and guidelines.  
- Shares information about school facilities.  
- Gives updates on extracurricular activities.  
- Announces school events and important notices.  
- Helps reduce the workload of school administrators by handling common queries instantly.
