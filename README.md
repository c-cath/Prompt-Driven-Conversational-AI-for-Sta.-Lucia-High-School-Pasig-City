Overview

The Sta. Lucia High School Chatbot, named Lucy, is a purpose-built conversational AI designed to serve the information needs of the Sta. Lucia High School (SLHS) community in Pasig City. It is a web-based application developed using Google AI Studio and the Gemini API.

Purpose

Lucy aims to provide instant, accurate, and user-friendly responses to common school-related inquiries, helping students, parents, teachers, and staff easily access important information while reducing the workload of school administrators.

Features

1. The chatbot can answer questions related to:
2. Academic programs and strands
3. Enrollment procedures and requirements
4. Faculty and staff information
5. Student policies and guidelines
6. School facilities
7. Extracurricular activities
8. School events and announcements
